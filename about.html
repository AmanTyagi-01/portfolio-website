Hi — I'm Aman, a computer science student at Jaipur National University with a deep passion for full-stack development and artificial intelligence.

I build things that live at the intersection of design and engineering — from intelligent Flask backends with NLP pipelines to clean, accessible frontend interfaces. My goal is always to ship software that's not just functional, but genuinely useful and polished.

My most recent project, an AI Resume Analyzer, uses TF-IDF cosine similarity and Google Gemini API integration to help people get smarter feedback on their resumes. It taught me as much about product thinking as it did about machine learning.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually diving deep into a research paper, contributing to open-source projects, or preparing presentations to share knowledge with peers.
